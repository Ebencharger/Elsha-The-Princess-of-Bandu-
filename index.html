ELSHA

The Princess of Bandu

ELSHA was a princess of Bandu kingdom, she eloped with a man whom she thought was a prince from another kingdom. Later she realized that the handsome prince was a luring demon. After the handsome prince successfully led Elsha to his territory having they both crossed many borders, he turned to demon and wanted to kill Elsha. Elsha ran out of the kingdom eventually in the custume of one of the kingdom's warriors, but to get back home, she must kill all the 10 border's keepers. They are not any easy creatures, no weapon has effect on their body, but only to some parts of their body. Will Elsha get back home? Only you can answer the question.
